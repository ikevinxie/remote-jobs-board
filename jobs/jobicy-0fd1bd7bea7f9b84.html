Job Overview

The Technical Problem Solver Expert leads the resolution of complex, high-impact technical issues using structured methodologies such as Shainin Red X and Six Sigma. This role drives measurable improvements in quality, cost, and delivery, while developing problem-solving capabilities across the organization.

Job Requirements

As a Technical Problem-Solving Expert, you will:

  • Solve complex and difficult technical problems
  • Lead a cross-functional team to apply diligently a structured problem-solving methodology (Shainin, Six Sigma or equivalent)
  • Define priorities
  • Proactively anticipate roadblocks to find solutions and escalate when needed
  • Ensure rigor and governance of Problem Solving methodologies across the organization
  • Coach and develop Problem Solver Practitioners
  • Select the projects that will have the highest impact across plants or business unit
  • Report out to Senior Management project progress, people development, savings

What your background should look like

Qualifications & Skills

  • Engineering degree (Physical, Mechanical, Electrical, Materials or related)
  • Shainin Red X Journeyman or Master certification (mandatory)
  • Six Sigma Black Belt or Master Black Belt preferred
  • 5–10 years’ experience in Technical Problem solving
  • Proven track record solving complex technical issues
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Leadership and cross-functional team management
  • Apply statistical analysis (Minitab, JMP) to validate root causes and/or solutions
  • Understanding of engineering fundamentals
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Willing to develop others
  • Be a positive change agent

 

Impact

  • Reduce Cost of Poor Quality (CoPQ)
  • Improve product quality and customer satisfaction
  • Strengthen the Problem-Solving culture
  • Deliver measurable business impact
