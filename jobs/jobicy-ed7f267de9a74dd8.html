The role

We are looking for an Instructional Designer to join our Nebius Academy team. In this position, you will be responsible for developing courses on Nebius AI Cloud for different target audiences.

Nebius Academy is an international online learning platform that helps individuals and teams master AI and cloud technologies. We offer hands-on, industry-relevant training for both B2C and B2B audiences, combining technical expertise with real-world application to deliver impactful, scalable learning experiences.

Please note that after applying for this position, you will be required to complete a test assignment. 

Your responsibilities will include: 

  • Participate in learning needs analysis and high-level course design with the Product Manager and SME.
  • Map learning outcomes for the whole course and for each module and lesson.
  • Translate SME materials into clear, learner-friendly educational content.
  • Design practice exercises and learning aids.
  • Collaborate with the production team to create visual content.
  • Audit existing courses for cognitive load, difficulty, and alignment with goals.
  • Work with student feedback and SME recommendations for continuous improvement.
  • Adapt course materials for different target audiences (onboarding, certification, etc.).
  • Maintain a backlog of improvements and keep course documentation up to date.

We expect you to have: 

  • At least 3 years of experience as an Instructional Designer or similar role.
  • Proven experience working with technical topics (software engineering, data science, etc.).
  • Deep knowledge of digital learning design frameworks and principles.
  • Ability to develop various educational content forms (long reads, video, assessments).
  • Proficiency in modern tools such as Figma, HeyGen, and Supademo.
  • Proficiency in leveraging AI tools such as ChatGPT to support content creation, research, and instructional design, while applying appropriate critical review and quality control. 
  • High level of English proficiency.

It will be an added bonus if you have: 

  • Experience with customer education and software instructions.
  • Experience with certification programs (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).
