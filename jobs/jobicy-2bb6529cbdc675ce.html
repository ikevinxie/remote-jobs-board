Project Manager I, IPCM (Remote)

Full time, United Kingdom, Denmark, Poland, Spain, Sweden

If you live in the Warsaw area, you will need to work in-office for 50% of working time monthly. If you work in the Kraków and Rzeszów areas, you will need to work in-office at least 1 day per week.

 

In this role, you will be responsible for delivering different types of projects involving (but not limited to) customer implementations and changes as well as agency projects, internal product implementations and core business process changes. You will be the primary role in delivering projects with a medium complexity, which is measured by the scale and scope of the request.

 

As a Project Manager I, IPCM, you will

  • Leverage regional and local resources and resources from the different departments and functions to support projects 

  • Follow all IPCM procedures and OneTouch Project methodology and reporting 

  • Manage Project risks 

  • Meet requirements of CSS IPCM Competency Matrix 

  • Responsible for local, regional and global project coordination with a broad scope of multi-national countries. Projects may include coordination of multiple countries at the same time

  • Understand client reporting requirements and data flows, Agency Operations end to end processes and resource and peer collaboration across functional teams 

  • Utilize proven project management methodology, tools and processes (PMP, OPPM, Prince2, Six Sigma) to scope, plan, analyze, initiate, execute, monitor and control projects 

 

About you

  • Proven experience in Project Management role

  • General to strong working knowledge of travel industry

  • Good written and oral communication skills 

  • Good technical skills in GDS preferred

  • Project Management certification (Prince2, PMP or equivalent) will be a plus
