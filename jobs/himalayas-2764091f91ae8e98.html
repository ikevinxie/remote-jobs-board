Description

Trimark is a leading innovator in utility-scale solutions to help electric power resources optimize control, connect to the grid, maximize revenue, and meter energy transactions. We specialize in state-of-the-art SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems and software platforms. Our cutting-edge technologies empower the renewable industry to monitor, control, and optimize complex processes with precision and efficiency. We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Project Manager with a foundational understanding of Scada Systems Integration (SI) Engineering and/or Operational Technology (OT). The ideal candidate will be responsible for planning, executing, and finalizing projects according to strict deadlines and within budget, while ensuring alignment with business goals and technical requirements.

This position may be performed remotely or based onsite or hybrid at our headquarters in Folsom, California, at management’s discretion.

Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage cross-functional project teams through the full project lifecycle.
  • Develop and maintain detailed project resource plans using Azure DevOps.
  • Monitor project progress, identify risks, and implement mitigation strategies.
  • Coordinate with SI team and project management team to ensure project alignment with project goals and capacity.
  • Communicate project status, risks, and issues to stakeholders and senior leadership.
  • Ensure compliance with internal standards, policies, and regulatory requirements.
  • Facilitate project meetings, document decisions, and track action items.
  • Support change management and training efforts related to project deliverables.

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Ability

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Information Technology, Business, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in project management, preferably in industrial or technology environments
  • Proven history of using excellent problem-solving skills to address project resource constraints and conflicts
  • Experience delivering projects utilizing both Waterfall and Agile methodologies
  • PMP, CSM, or other related certifications
  • Strong communication, organizational, and stakeholder management skills
  • Must be legally authorized to work in the United States of America. This position is not eligible for Visa sponsorship
