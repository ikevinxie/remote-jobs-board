Our AI platform gets clinicians the patient answers they need fast. We have paying customers using it daily. This is an early engineering hire on our Platform team (5–7 people), reporting to me. No prior Temporal experience needed, just enthusiasm for it. How we work: strong code-review and pairing culture, serious about testing, async-first with a single daily sync, and we've fully integrated plan/spec-based AI tooling into our workflow (Claude, Cursor, CodeRabbit, Copilot — your choice). Looking for 3–5+ yrs full-stack experience, solid Python + a backend framework, comfort with relational DBs, a modern JS framework, real testing chops, and someone who thrives with ambiguity and owns problems end to end. Healthcare/FHIR/HL7 and AI-feature experience are nice-to-haves, not requirements. Must be in/near NYC for the weekly in-person day.
