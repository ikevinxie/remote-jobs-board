We are working with a multinational financial services company specialising in online trading who is seeking to onboard an Affiliate Manager to join their team.

Position: Affiliate Manager

Location: Remote in EU, or Israel

Employment type: Full-time

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Identify, recruit, and onboard new affiliate partners while maintaining strong relationships with existing ones.
  • Oversee and optimize the performance of affiliate programs and day-to-day activities.
  • Respond to affiliate and partner inquiries, resolving account-related issues efficiently.
  • Research and negotiate with affiliate networks and traffic partners to expand reach and align with business goals.
  • Draft and negotiate partnership agreements, renewals, and performance-based contracts.
  • Develop partnership proposals based on expected return on investment (ROI) and performance metrics.
  • Monitor affiliate activity for compliance with program policies, including fraud detection and adherence to guidelines.
  • Analyze campaign performance and sales data to evaluate effectiveness and suggest improvements.
  • Study market trends and demographics to identify new opportunities for growth and product promotion.
  • Collaborate with creative and marketing teams to develop promotional materials tailored to specific regions and audiences.
  • Write marketing briefs and contribute to the development of advertising assets such as banners, landing pages, and email templates.
  • Propose and launch new campaign initiatives, bonus structures, and promotional strategies for affiliates.
  • Represent the company at industry events, conferences, and networking meetups.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fluent in English, with strong proficiency in both written and spoken communication. Additional languages are a bonus.
  • Strong understanding of CYSEC regulations is required
  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, Digital Media, or a related field (preferred).
  • 1-2 years of experience in affiliate marketing or partnership management.
  • Industry experience in digital marketing, fintech, or similar sectors is a plus.
  • Solid understanding of affiliate marketing, partner networks, and digital acquisition strategies.
  • Familiarity with compliance and marketing regulations is advantageous.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel; knowledge of affiliate platforms and tracking tools is desirable.
  • Strong analytical skills with attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple projects.
  • Proactive, self-motivated, and able to meet tight deadlines independently.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with a collaborative mindset.
  • Willingness to travel occasionally for events, conferences, or team meetings.

OFFER:

  • Generous paid annual leave.
  • Comprehensive health insurance plan with family coverage.
  • Access to online learning platforms for continuous development.
  • Employee discounts on various products and services.
  • Regular company events and team-building activities.
  • Opportunities for professional growth in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.
