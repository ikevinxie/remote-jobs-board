The Role:

 

We are looking for a Senior Salesforce Developer with experience to join one of our fast-growing client teams. You will play a key role in building modern web applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and helping shape the future of user experiences across various platforms.

 

What we’re looking for:

 

  • Minimum of 4 years of experience in Salesforce development.
  • Deep understanding of Salesforce architecture, data model, and security model.
  • Strong hands-on experience with Apex, Visualforce, Lightning Web Components (LWC), and SOQL.
  • Proven experience developing custom Salesforce applications and integrations using REST and SOAP APIs.
  • Experience with Salesforce Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and Experience Cloud.
  • Familiarity with CI/CD tools and development lifecycle in Salesforce (SFDX, Git, DX CLI, etc.).
  • Salesforce Platform Developer I certification (Developer II is a strong plus).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to translate business requirements into scalable solutions.
  • Strong communication skills in English, both written and verbal.
  • Located in CET timezone (+/- 3 hours), we are unable to consider applications from candidates in other time zones.

 

Nice-to-have

 

  • Experience with third-party Salesforce apps (e.g., Conga, DocuSign, CPQ, etc.).
  • Familiarity with Salesforce DevOps tools like Copado or Gearset.
  • Understanding of large-scale data migration, data quality, and deduplication strategies.
  • Exposure to Agile/Scrum methodologies and tools like Jira or Asana.
  • Knowledge of industry-specific Salesforce implementations (e.g., Financial Services Cloud, Health Cloud).

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Design and develop scalable Salesforce solutions that align with business requirements.
  • Write clean, efficient, and well-documented Apex code and Lightning components.
  • Build and maintain integrations with external systems using APIs and middleware.
  • Customize Salesforce objects, fields, workflows, validation rules, and process automation.
  • Optimize performance and ensure platform scalability and security.
  • Collaborate with admins, analysts, architects, and stakeholders to deliver impactful features.
  • Conduct code reviews and provide technical guidance to fellow developers.
  • Stay current with Salesforce platform updates, releases, and best practices.
  • Participate in technical planning, estimation, and solution design discussions.
