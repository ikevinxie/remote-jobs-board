Who we're looking for

    We’re looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our engineering team! They will be a key contributor to Ethena’s growing product surface area and engineering operations.

    Engineering Manager

What you'll do

  • Develop full stack software using Typescript, MySQL, React, and Next.js.

  • Direct AI coding agent(s) to assist you in building great things quickly, while maintaining a production quality codebase.

  • Own features big and small from the idea phase through to production

  • Collaborate with other engineers, designers, product managers, and members of the wider Ethena team.

What makes a great candidate

  • A hunger for building great software faster than ever using AI coding agents, along with an understanding of the pitfalls and how to avoid them.

  • A great knack for simple and elegant system design.

  • Deep expertise building and maintaining great web applications, especially in a B2B context.

  • Comfort with Javascript/Typescript, MySQL, React, Next.js, and AWS.

  • Extensive experience shipping agile software sustainably (Git, CI/CD, database migrations, logging & monitoring, infrastructure considerations, etc).
