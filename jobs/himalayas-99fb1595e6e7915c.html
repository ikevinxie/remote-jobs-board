The Role

The Senior Construction Engineer reports to the Manager of Engineering and plays a pivotal role in the design, planning, and execution of complex infrastructure, telecommunications, and building projects. This role requires deep technical expertise, a passion for innovation, and a strong commitment to safety and engineering excellence.

The successful candidate will combine strong engineering expertise with innovative thinking to deliver scalable, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions while ensuring compliance with all safety, quality, and regulatory requirements. The role requires leadership of cross-functional teams, engagement with external engineering and construction partners, and the application of emerging technologies and construction methodologies to improve project outcomes.

Location

This is a work from home position within the U.S.

The Main Responsibilities

  • Lead a multidisciplinary team of engineers, project managers, and solution architects for multiple large-scale telecommunications and infrastructure projects.
  • Lead solution development involving network architecture, civil engineering, site development, outside plant (OSP) infrastructure, mechanical, electrical, and prime power systems.
  • Create complex Scopes of Work, Architectural & Engineering (A&E) designs, and Construction Drawings (CDs) as well as project schedules, budgets, resource requirements, risk mitigation plans, governances, and performance metrics.
  • Ensure projects are delivered safely, on schedule, within budget, and in accordance with company standards and comply with applicable codes, regulations, permitting requirements, and industry standards.
  • Oversee route planning, fiber assignments, network capacity requirements, and infrastructure acceptance activities.

What We Look For in a Candidate

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Construction Management, or a related technical discipline; or equivalent combination of education and relevant experience.
  • Minimum 8 years of engineering, construction, telecommunications, or infrastructure project experience.
  • Demonstrated experience leading complex engineering and construction projects through the full project lifecycle.
  • Strong knowledge of construction management principles, engineering practices, project controls, and safety requirements.
  • Experience managing external engineering firms, contractors, and construction vendors.
  • Working knowledge of OSP engineering standards and telecommunications network deployment.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications including Excel, Word, and Project.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and perform effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Professional Engineer (PE) license or equivalent professional certification.
  • Experience with telecommunications fiber network design and deployment.
  • Experience with civil engineering and site development projects.
  • Knowledge of Lean Construction, Design-Build, or Integrated Project Delivery (IPD) methodologies.
  • Familiarity with digital construction platforms such as Autodesk Construction Cloud, Procore, or similar systems.
  • Experience implementing innovative construction technologies and sustainable design practices.
  • Understanding of sustainability frameworks, energy-efficient infrastructure, and carbon reduction initiatives.
