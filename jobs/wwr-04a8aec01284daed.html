What You’ll Be Doing (Job Tasks)

You’ll be the architect & Copy Chief of our copy across the entire Vidalytics marketing engine. 

While you may work on all of our copy and content the key to success in this role is getting copy to convert on cold traffic / cold email / ads / etc. You need to have direct response, conversion minded best practices in your DNA.

  • Cold email

  • Split tests (from the copy side — you don’t run the ads/tests yourself, but you create and iterate the copy being tested)

  • Marketing website

  • Onboarding emails

  • Content marketing, AEO content, product tooltips, and other basic in-app copy

  • Social posts

  • Copy that touches work owned by our Product team

 

You’ll work shoulder-to-shoulder with the marketing team, and you’ll own the hypothesis/testing framework that tells us what’s actually winning. Basically you need to be a conversion detective. 

You’ll need to completely understand all the key marketing info about our biz - such as the product, social proof, customer journey, etc..
