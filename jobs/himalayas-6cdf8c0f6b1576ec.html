    • About the Host & Role Overview﻿

      Our host company operates within the various but relevant sector and provides valuable opportunities for graduates and emerging professionals to gain practical industry experience in their field where you get an opportunity to gain hands-on experience, develop industry-relevant skills, and work alongside experienced professionals in a real workplace environment (onsite, remote, or project-based). ﻿

      Key Learning Responsibilities﻿

      • Technical Support: Provide assistance with hardware and software troubleshooting, including desktops, laptops, and peripheral devices such as printers and scanners​.
      • Network Support: Assist in basic network maintenance, resolving connectivity issues, and managing network devices like routers and switches​
      • Documentation: Create and update user guides, technical documentation, and knowledge base articles for internal use​.
      • User Support: Respond to and resolve user queries related to operating systems, software, and other IT-related issues via email, phone, or in-person​.
      • Asset Management: Track and manage IT inventory, ensuring accurate record-keeping and timely updates.
      • System Monitoring: Monitor and log system activities to identify and resolve potential problems​.

      Skills & Attributes

      • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
      • Robust communication and interpersonal skills
      • Ability to work collaboratively
      • Good organisational and time management skills
      • Relevant educational background, training, or interest in the industry

      Benefits of our Voluntary Professional Career Internship Program

      • Hands-on industry exposure to bridge the gap between education and employment with flexibility on a part time basis.
      • There may be a high potential for securing a job following the completion of the internship for the right talent.
      • The placement fee is the lowest possible and is only if a successful placement is secured; there are no registration fees.
      • You will have the opportunity to work alongside professionals in your industry, expanding your network, competencies, and career growth.
      • This internship offers a wealth of knowledge and transferable skills that you can gain, and the host company will provide you with a work experience letter.
