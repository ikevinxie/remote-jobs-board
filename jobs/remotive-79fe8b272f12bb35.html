 Job Title: Sales Assistant

 

 Company: Endureed

 

 Location: Lake City, FL / Remote eligible

 

 Reports to: VP of Global Sales

 

 

ABOUT THE ROLE

 

 

The Sales Assistant is the operational backbone of the US sales team. You'll provide hands-on administrative, coordination, and customer support to the VP of Global Sales — keeping the department running smoothly, accurately, and on time across residential and Strategic Partner (SP) channels.

 

 

 

This isn't a role for someone who wants to wing it, work independently without structure, or stay at arm's length from the details. It's a role for someone who finds real satisfaction in making sure nothing falls through the cracks someone who takes pride in accuracy, follows through without being asked twice, and genuinely enjoys being the person others can count on.

 

 

 

If you're the kind of person who gets energized by creating order, supporting a team, and doing the unglamorous-but-critical work that makes everything else possible keep reading.

 

 

 

✓  You'll thrive here if you...

 

•       Get genuine satisfaction from organizing, tracking, and keeping things accurate

 

•       Take ownership of tasks and follow through without being reminded

 

•       Prefer a defined role with clear expectations over open-ended autonomy

 

•       Are naturally calm, steady, and don't rattle easily when things get busy

 

•       Build trust by being dependable, not by being loud

 

•       Take pride in serving others and helping your team look good

 

•       Are bilingual in English and Spanish and comfortable using both professionally

 

•       Thrive in a structured, process-driven environment with consistent routines

 

✗  This role is NOT for you if you...

 

•       Need to be the center of attention or get credit to feel motivated

 

•       Prefer creating your own process over following an established one

 

•       Dislike repetitive tasks or detailed administrative work

 

•       Get bored or frustrated when you're not driving strategy or making big decisions

 

•       Struggle to stay organized when managing multiple people's priorities at once

 

•       Want flexibility to define your own schedule or work style without check-ins

 

•       Move fast and loose, accuracy and follow-through aren't your strong suits
