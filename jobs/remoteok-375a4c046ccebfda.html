About Intelligent Change

Intelligent Change exists to help people design better lives through small, consistent rituals that compound over time.

We create physical tools and experiences that turn personal growth into something people can actually live. Not through pressure or motivation, but through structure, reflection, and intention.

We are the creators of the most viral journal in the world, The Five Minute Journal, a product that helped turn gratitude into a daily practice for millions of people globally. To date, Intelligent Change has sold over 4,000,000 products worldwide and built a deeply loyal, values-driven community.

Our products are used and recommended by Tim Ferriss, Emma Watson, Lily Collins, professional athletes, CEOs, founders, and leaders across industries, alongside millions of everyday people seeking more intentional lives.

Intelligent Change has been featured in The New York Times, Vogue, Forbes, ELLE, and other leading global publications. The New York Times described Intelligent Change as a brand that has "turned gratitude into a global movement."

The Role

We're looking for a Designer with exceptional visual taste and a passion for creating work that feels timeless, thoughtful, and emotionally resonant.

This role reports directly to the Creative Director and sits at the heart of our creative team. You'll primarily design for social media and organic channels, bringing our products and ideas to life through engaging visual storytelling across Instagram, email, and digital campaigns.

You'll also collaborate closely with our Product Development team to design packaging, printed products, marketing materials, and launch assets that extend our brand across every customer touchpoint.

This is a role for someone who cares deeply about craft, typography, composition, photography, and the details that make great design feel effortless.

Remote Role

This is a full-time, fully remote contract role.

We're open to candidates worldwide, provided you can work overlapping hours with our team, who operate across ET and GMT time zones.

Candidates based in Toronto or Montreal will be given preference, as you'll occasionally join the Creative Director on set for campaign and content shoots.

What You'll Do

Design Across Social

  • Create high-quality designs for Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, Meta ads, email, and web
  • Work closely with the Creative & Social teams to develop engaging social-first visuals that balance performance with strong brand design
  • Design static posts, carousels, stories, reels covers, motion concepts, and campaign assets

Shape the Brand

  • Help evolve and maintain a consistent visual identity across every customer touchpoint
  • Contribute ideas for campaigns, launches, seasonal moments, and new creative directions
  • Uphold a high bar for design quality and visual consistency

Design Physical Products

  • Support the Creative Director with design on packaging, journals, cards, books, and other physical products
  • Design supporting collateral including inserts, retail displays, presentations, and launch materials
  • Prepare production-ready artwork for print

Collaborate Across Teams

  • Work closely with the Creative Director to bring concepts from idea through execution
  • Partner with marketing, product, copywriting, and operations teams to deliver cohesive campaigns
  • Give and receive thoughtful creative feedback

Stay Curious

  • Bring references, trends, ideas, and inspiration from fashion, publishing, interiors, wellness, and culture
  • Continuously refine your craft and contribute fresh creative thinking to the team

Who You Are

Exceptional Visual Taste

You have a strong design point of view and a portfolio that demonstrates thoughtful typography, layout, composition, and attention to detail.

Social-First Designer

You understand what performs on modern social platforms without sacrificing quality or brand integrity. You know how to create content that feels native, engaging, and highly shareable.

Product Minded

You enjoy designing physical objects as much as digital ones. Packaging, books, journals, and printed materials excite you.

Curious and Collaborative

You enjoy working closely with writers, marketers, videographers, photographers, and product developers.

You communicate well, ask thoughtful questions, and care about making the work better.

Values Aligned

You have a genuine interest in mindfulness, personal growth, psychology, wellness, or intentional living. Our products resonate with you because you believe in what they help people achieve.

Detail Obsessed

You notice the spacing. The typography. The kerning. The print finish. The things most people overlook.

What We're Looking For

  • Please note: Only candidates with submitted portfolios will be considered
  • 2+ years of experience in graphic design or brand design
  • Outstanding portfolio demonstrating strong visual taste
  • Experience designing for social media
  • Strong typography and editorial design skills
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva, Capcut
  • Experience with AI such as Claude, Midjourney and other AI platforms
  • Experience designing packaging or printed products is a significant advantage
  • Motion design experience is a bonus
  • Photography, art direction, or editing experience is a bonus
