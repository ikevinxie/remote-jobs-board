About the role

RubyLabs is looking for a Superhero Animator to join our creative squad and own premium, web-native animations for one of our fastest-growing portfolio products — an online sweepstakes social casino. We need "wow" quality, at a daily production cadence, built in Rive as interactive, state-driven animations (not pre-rendered video). This is a senior, high-ownership role with room to grow into a leadership role for a small animation pod as the team scales.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and animate character mascots (idle loops, reactions, win celebrations) that feel alive and premium

  • Build hero-section animations combining characters with animated environments (coins, slot machines, effects)

  • Rig in Rive using state machines so animations react live to real product events

  • Run a daily/weekly production pipeline for new offer animations across segments

  • Partner with dev on responsive embedding and with design on the avatar art pipeline

Qualifications

  • 4+ years of experience in the online casino / iGaming space

  • 3+ years of experience with online casino & online gaming animation and motion designs production with characters that are specific to the online casino themes

  • Highly proficient with AI animation tools (e.g., Viggle, Kling, Runway) for rapid concepting and production — be ready to walk through a real project where you used one in production, including where it broke down

  • Experience running high-intensity motion production and delivering results fast

  • Experience of a highly professional studio-level motion production design

Nice to have

  • Hands-on production experience in Rive (or Spine/DragonBones with fast ramp-up ability), with character animation in your portfolio

  • Experience building reusable rigged character skeletons with multiple states

  • Particle/VFX experience (coin bursts, win effects)
