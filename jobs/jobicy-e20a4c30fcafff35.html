MANTECH seeks a motivated, career and customer-oriented Junior Procurement Analyst to join our team in Halawa, HI.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • The Junior Procurement Analyst supports the coordination of procurement services by assisting with document preparation and tracking the status of open purchase orders.
  • They help monitor the delivery status of materials and ensure that records are accurately maintained within organizational databases.
  • They assist in preparing procurement schedules and ensure that necessary materials and data are available for senior analysts.
  • The Junior Procurement Analyst drafts technical records for procurement tasks and assists in the coordination of activities across theater locations.
  • They maintain foundational knowledge of special operations requirements and assist in preparing documentation for program deliverables.
  • They help monitor regional vendor conditions and assist senior expeditors in identifying routine supply and service needs.
  • The Junior Procurement Analyst takes direction, guidance, and mentoring from more senior staff.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or a related field.
  • Demonstrated ability to follow established procurement SOPs and maintain accurate technical records.

Desired Qualifications:

  • Foundational knowledge of FAR/DFARS regulations or a related field.
  • Basic proficiency in Microsoft Excel for tracking procurement metrics.
  • Familiarity with basic logistics and supply chain systems.

Clearance Requirements:

  • Must have a current/active Secret security clearance

Physical Requirements:

  • Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50%.
  • Constantly operates a computer and other office productivity machinery, such as a calculator, copy machine and computer printer.
  • The person in this position frequently communicates with co-workers, management and customers, which may involve delivering presentations. Must be able to exchange accurate information in these situations.
