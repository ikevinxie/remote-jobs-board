Content Creator (100% remote)

🏢 Marker Video · 📍 USA or Canada only · 市场营销 | Marketing · 美洲 | Americas · 来源 Working Nomads · 发布 2026-07-06
去原站申请 | Apply on Working Nomads ↗

Make videos about brands you love (eg: HelloFresh) and get paid (no followers required!)

Hello! We’re looking for everyday people with something to say to join our platform!👋

Sign up (it's free) and create videos about the brands you love across beauty, food & drink, lifestyle, fitness, and travel.

Brands actively using Marker to buy authentic videos include: HelloFresh, Dove, Vaseline, and L'Oreal!

Create videos in your own tone of voice and style; there are no briefs or brand interventions here. Authenticity and videos filled with your unique perspective are what brands need and want.

Videos can be reviews, tutorials, first impressions, unboxing vlogs, etc. Just keep them to 30 seconds or less! 

Once your video has been uploaded and approved on Marker, the brand featured can buy and use it for their marketing. You’ll earn up to $100 for each video you sell on our platform, and you can submit videos as often as you like (the more you upload, the more you can earn). 

To sign up, ensure you are: 

  • Based only in the USA or Canada (although you submit videos remotely, we are only Stripe-enabled in these territories, so if you sign up from elsewhere, we will NOT be able to verify or pay you).

  • Comfortable in front of the camera

  • Fluent in spoken & written English

  • A strong, confident storyteller 

  • Competent in recording, editing and uploading social media videos

  • Seriously interested in TikTok & Instagram

  • Passionate about brands and products, and the content on their social channels 

 

Note that this is not a full-time role. Upload videos as and when you choose, and you’ll be paid for each piece of content that you sell on Marker.

Please register via the provided link ONLY; note that any applicants who email us to apply will not be registered on our system.

We can’t wait to hear from you! 
Team Marker
