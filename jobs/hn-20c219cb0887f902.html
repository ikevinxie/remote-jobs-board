Colonist | Senior Product Engineer, QA Engineer | Full-time | Remote & async Colonist (colonist.io) is a multiplayer board game platform. ~20 people, millions of monthly players, no middle management between you and the product. You'll own features end-to-end and ship to a live player base within days. Right now we're evolving the core experience and building toward a multi-game platform. The mission is to become the internet's biggest board game platform. This role is a good fit if you: want to see your work matter at real scale without a large team around you, are comfortable with ambiguity and don't need someone to define your tasks, and use AI tools as a genuine multiplier in your workflow. This isn't a good fit if you: prefer a larger team with specialists for every layer, or are looking for a role with clearly scoped tickets. More on the roles, culture and values: https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/colonist
