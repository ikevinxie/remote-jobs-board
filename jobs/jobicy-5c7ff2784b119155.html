Position Overview

 

dormakaba is seeking an Inside Sales Representative that is remotely based or in our Lexington, KY office. This role is for our Safe Locks business, a leading global provider of high-security locking systems for commercial, banking, retail, and ATM applications. The Inside Sales Representative is responsible for supporting new business development, inbound order requests, and day-to-day requirements for our clients.

 

HIRING SALARY RANGE: Base Salary $50,000 - $65,000 (Salary to be determined by the education, experience, knowledge, skills, and abilities of the applicant, internal equity, and alignment with market data.) Plus, a generous Sales Incentive Compensation. This position includes a competitive benefits package. Please visit our career site for more information on benefits.

 

What you will do

  • Drive profitable sales growth through new business development efforts as well as increasing expanding business with existing clients
  • Solidify reputation as a trusted advisor deploying consultative approach to generating new business
  • Identify strategic opportunities and establish sales tactics to penetrate and develop specific clients in assigned regions and markets
  • Continuously review competitive market landscape and recommend, develop, and implement new and creative tactics to maximize sales opportunities and revenue
  • Perform effectively within a cross-functional team that is comprised of both internal and external stakeholders

 

What we require

  • 2+ years of Account Management/Inside Sales experience
  • Travel up to 25% (customers, training, and trade shows)
  • High school diploma

 

What we prefer

  • Bachelor’s degree
  • Knowledge or experience with manufacturing, ATM’s, Financial Institutions, armored transportation, or related field
  • Experience with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software (Pipedrive)
  • Familiarity with Spanish
